About Respiratory Diseases

Respiratory diseases such as asthma and COPD are serious health issues affecting millions worldwide. Early detection and proper management are crucial for maintaining respiratory health.

Environmental factors like air quality, humidity, and temperature can significantly impact respiratory health. This is where RespiGuard comes in, combining sound analysis with environmental data to provide users with actionable insights.
